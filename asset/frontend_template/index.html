Prestigious Landmarks

Shanta Holdings Limited (SHL) has emerged as the leading Real Estate developer with a mission to change the lifestyle of city dwellers, by providing functional and aesthetical living & working spaces that are comparable internationally.

Our philosophy is to build prestigious commercial & residential complexes in prime locations, which are true landmarks on the skyline. Utmost consideration is given to the areas of architecture, lifestyle facilities, safety (physical, fire & electro mechanical), luxury and comfort.

Superior Quality and Architecture

Quality is our core principle and architecture is our passion. We endeavor to offer global standard architectural design solutions whilst keeping the local culture in mind. We believe that every project is unique; hence unique architectural requirements may arise. Thus, we work very closely with a panel of the leading Architects to sketch your dreams into reality. While our highly experienced Engineering & Quality Assurance teams ensure those dreams are built to unparalleled quality.

Best Construction Materials

At SHL, we use the best construction materials without compromise, such as 72 grade Steel; highest grade Portland Composite Cement of only multinational brands; Sylhet Sand for casting (min FM2.5) or Fine Local Sand for finishing works; best quality Ready Mix Concrete (RMC); and custom machine made Solid Cement Concrete Block (CCB)  to ensure zero salinity.

For finishing works, we continuously explore design & material sourcing globally to enhance the comfort and lifestyle of our target customers and joint venture partners. Each material used in our projects is selected with the utmost attention to quality,  suitability and durability.

World-Class Consultants

As part of our commitment to achieving excellence in product quality we engage world-class consultants, both foreign and local, at different stages and areas of development such as Structural Design, Architecture & Interior Design,  Electro Mechanical  Engineering (EME), Plumbing & Fire Fighting, Landscaping & Lighting and others depending on the specifics of the project.

Uncompromised Safety

Our priority to safety is second to none, especially in the area of  Electro Mechanical  design and equipment selection such as Elevators, Generators, Substations, as well as Firefighting systems, which are designed by the most reputed EME consultants and only sourced from the world’s leading state-of-the-art brands.

On-Time Delivery


Our commitment to our joint venture partners and customers are absolute in terms of quality and on time delivery. Though a project requires extensive effort in every aspect of its development, especially the design and construction phases, we at SHL always believe efficient project management is key for on time completion.

That is why an expert team of highly qualified Engineers and management professionals work relentlessly to ensure this objective.
